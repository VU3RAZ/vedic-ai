=== VEDIC HOROSCOPE ANALYSIS ALGORITHM ===
Based on: B.V. Raman - "How to Judge a Horoscope" (Volumes 1 & 2)
For Vector Database Embedding

--- MODULE 1: FOUNDATION SETUP ---

STEP 1.1: CHART CONSTRUCTION
- Calculate Lagna (Ascendant) based on birth time, date, and place
- Determine Rasi chart (D1) with all 12 houses
- Calculate Navamsa chart (D9) - crucial secondary divisional chart
- Note planetary longitudes in Nirayana (sidereal) system
- Identify: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu positions
- Calculate planetary degrees, sign occupancy, house occupancy

STEP 1.2: DETERMINE FUNCTIONAL NATURE OF PLANETS (Per Lagna)
- Aries Lagna: Jupiter/Sun/Mars = benefics; Saturn/Mercury/Venus = malefics
- Taurus Lagna: Saturn = best benefic; Mercury/Mars/Sun = benefics; Jupiter/Venus/Moon = malefics
- Gemini Lagna: Venus = sole benefic; Mars = greatest malefic; Jupiter/Sun = evil; Moon/Mercury = neutrals
- Cancer Lagna: Jupiter/Mars = benefics; Venus/Mercury = evil; Saturn/Moon/Sun = neutrals
- Leo Lagna: Mars/Sun = benefics; Mercury/Venus = malefics; Jupiter/Moon/Saturn = neutrals
- Virgo Lagna: Venus = best benefic; Moon/Mars/Jupiter = malefics; Saturn/Sun/Mercury = neutrals
- Libra Lagna: Saturn/Mercury/Venus = benefics; Sun/Jupiter/Moon = malefics; Mars = feeble benefic
- Scorpio Lagna: Moon = best benefic; Jupiter/Sun = benefics; Mercury/Venus = evil; Mars/Saturn = neutrals
- Sagittarius Lagna: Mars/Sun = benefics; Venus/Saturn/Mercury = evil; Jupiter/Moon = neutrals
- Capricorn Lagna: Venus = most powerful benefic; Mercury/Saturn = benefics; Mars/Jupiter/Moon = evil
- Aquarius Lagna: Venus = benefic; Sun/Mars = benefics; Jupiter/Moon = malefics; Mercury = neutral
- Pisces Lagna: Moon/Mars = benefics; Saturn/Sun/Venus/Mercury = malefics; Jupiter = neutral

STEP 1.3: IDENTIFY YOGAKARAKAS
- Planet owning both Kendra (1,4,7,10) AND Trikona (1,5,9) = Yogakaraka
- Most powerful Rajayoga: 9th+10th lord association/exchange/aspect
- Other strong combinations: 4th+5th, 7th+5th, 10th+5th, 4th+9th, 7th+9th lords
- Yogakaraka requires strength within 12 degrees for full effect
- Natural malefic (Mars/Saturn) as Kendra lord = benefic functionally

--- MODULE 2: ASSESS PLANETARY STRENGTHS ---

STEP 2.1: DIGNITIES AND DEBILITIES
For each planet, assess:
- Exaltation (highest strength) -> Sun: Aries, Moon: Taurus, Mars: Capricorn, Mercury: Virgo, Jupiter: Cancer, Venus: Pisces, Saturn: Libra
- Own sign / Moolatrikona (strong)
- Friendly sign (moderate)
- Neutral sign (average)
- Enemy sign (weak)
- Debilitation (lowest) -> Sun: Libra, Moon: Scorpio, Mars: Cancer, Mercury: Pisces, Jupiter: Capricorn, Venus: Virgo, Saturn: Aries
- Retrograde = additional strength (triple in some methods)
- Vargottama (same sign in Rasi and Navamsa) = double strength

STEP 2.2: NEECHABHANGA (CANCELLATION OF DEBILITY)
Debility is cancelled if:
- Lord of exaltation sign is in Kendra from Lagna or Moon
- Sign-dispositor of debilitated planet is in Kendra from Lagna
- Debilitated planet is aspected by its exaltation lord
- Debilitated planet exchanges signs with its sign lord

STEP 2.3: COMBUSTION (ASTANGATA)
Orbs for combustion:
- Mars: 17°, Mercury: 14° (12° if retrograde), Jupiter: 11°
- Venus: 10° (8° if retrograde), Saturn: 5°
- Venus and Saturn are EXEMPT from combustion reduction
- Combust planets lose half their period in Amsayu calculation

STEP 2.4: PAPAKARTARI / SUBHAKARTARI YOGA
- Papakartari: House/planet hemmed between malefics (weakens significations)
- Subhakartari: House/planet hemmed between benefics (strengthens significations)

--- MODULE 3: HOUSE ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK ---

STEP 3.1: THREE-FACTOR ANALYSIS PER BHAVA
For EACH of the 12 houses, analyze:
A) THE HOUSE itself: Sign, occupants, aspects received
B) THE LORD: Placement, strength, aspects, associations
C) THE KARAKA (natural significator):
   - 1st: Sun; 2nd: Jupiter; 3rd: Mars; 4th: Moon/Venus
   - 5th: Jupiter; 6th: Mars/Saturn; 7th: Venus; 8th: Saturn
   - 9th: Jupiter/Sun; 10th: Sun/Mercury/Jupiter/Saturn; 11th: Jupiter; 12th: Saturn/Ketu

STEP 3.2: HOUSE ANALYSIS CHECKLIST
For each house:
□ Sign occupying the house (benefic/malefic sign?)
□ Planets in the house (benefic/malefic/neutral occupants?)
□ Aspects the house receives (from which planets/houses?)
□ House lord placement (which house, sign, strength level?)
□ Lord in exaltation/own sign/debility?
□ Aspects received by lord (benefic aspects help, malefic aspects hurt)
□ Lord's associations (conjunctions - most powerful influence)
□ Karaka's condition and placement
□ Assess BOTH from Lagna AND Chandra Lagna (Moon as ascendant)
□ Check in Navamsa chart
□ Is lord in 6th, 8th, or 12th from the house? (adverse)
□ Is lord in 6th/8th/12th in Navamsa? (adverse timing)

STEP 3.3: KEY POSITIONAL OUTCOMES FOR HOUSE LORD
Favorable: 1, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10, 11 = positive indications
Unfavorable: 6, 8, 12 = weakens or reverses house indications
Special: Own house placement = self-reliant, strong indications

--- MODULE 4: HOUSE-BY-HOUSE DETAILED ANALYSIS ---

STEP 4.1: FIRST HOUSE (Lagna/Thanu Bhava)
Significations: Body, personality, health, life conditions, physical appearance, vitality
Analysis:
- Lagna sign and its nature (movable/fixed/dual, element, ruler)
- Lagna lord's house placement and strength
- Planets conjunct Lagna (most powerful physical/personality influence)
- Aspects received on Lagna (malefic aspects = health concerns)
Health triad: Assess Sun (soul/vitality), Moon (mind), Lagna (body) together
Strong Lagna + strong Sun + strong Moon = excellent constitution

STEP 4.2: SECOND HOUSE (Dhana Bhava)
Significations: Wealth, family, speech, right eye, food, manner of death
Karaka: Jupiter
Analysis:
- 2nd lord placement and associations
- Jupiter strength
- Malefics in 2nd = expense of wealth or family troubles
- 2nd house = one of two primary Maraka (death) houses

STEP 4.3: THIRD HOUSE (Sahaja Bhava)
Significations: Siblings, courage, short travel, communication, ears/throat
Karaka: Mars
Analysis:
- 3rd lord in different houses gives varied sibling and courage outcomes
- Mars condition = sibling relationships
- Malefics in 3rd = troubled siblings but enhances courage
- 3rd lord in 6/8/12 = loss or harm to siblings

STEP 4.4: FOURTH HOUSE (Sukha Bhava)
Significations: Mother, home, education, property, vehicles, peace of mind
Karaka: Moon (mother), Venus (vehicles), Mercury (education)
Analysis:
- 4th lord and Moon strength
- Venus for property/vehicles
- Malefics in 4th = unhappy home life, troubled mother
- Strong 4th = comforts, good education, happiness

STEP 4.5: FIFTH HOUSE (Putra Bhava)
Significations: Children, intelligence, speculation, romance, past-life merits
Karaka: Jupiter (Putrakaraka)
Analysis:
- 5th lord strength and Jupiter's condition
- Beeja Sphuta (male fertility point): Sum of Sun + Venus + Jupiter longitudes ÷ 360 remainder → should be odd sign and odd Navamsa, aspected by benefics
- Kshetra Sphuta (female fertility point): Sum of Jupiter + Mars + Moon longitudes ÷ 360 remainder → should be even sign and even Navamsa
- Assess 5th from Lagna, Moon, and Jupiter
- Afflicted 5th = difficulty with children or intelligence issues

STEP 4.6: SIXTH HOUSE (Ripu/Roga Bhava)
Significations: Enemies, diseases, debts, subordinates, litigation, service
Karaka: Mars/Saturn
Analysis:
- 6th lord placement (in 6th = strong for overcoming enemies)
- Disease indicators: planets in 6/8/12, aspects on Lagna lord
- Tridosha: Sun/Mars = pitta; Moon/Venus = kapha; Saturn = vata; Mercury = mixed
- Mental health: Moon and Mercury assessment (see STEP 8.3)

STEP 4.7: SEVENTH HOUSE (Kalatra Bhava)
Significations: Marriage, spouse, partnerships, sexual urges, business partners
Karaka: Venus (Kalatrakaraka)
Analysis:
Primary factors: (a) 7th house, (b) 7th lord, (c) Venus
Secondary factors: 7th house occupants, planets aspecting 7th lord, 7th lord from Moon, karaka Venus

Marriage quality:
+ Happy: 7th in benefic sign, strong 7th lord, strong Venus, benefics in 2/7/11 from 7th lord
+ Happy: Jupiter/Venus aspecting 7th; 7th lord in Kendra/Trikona
- Unhappy: Malefics in 7th unaspected by benefics; 7th lord in 6/8/12
- Death of spouse: Malefics in 4/8/12 from Venus; 5th/8th lord in 7th; Venus hemmed by malefics

Timing of marriage:
- Dasha/Bhukti of: 7th lord, Venus, planets occupying 7th, planets aspecting 7th
- 7th lord from Moon also operates

Kuja Dosha:
- Mars in 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th from Lagna/Moon/Venus
- Strength order: 8th > 7th > 4th > 2nd > 12th
- Exempt: Mars in Leo/Aquarius; Mars conjunct Jupiter/Moon; specific sign exemptions per house

STEP 4.8: EIGHTH HOUSE (Ayus Bhava)
Significations: Longevity, occult, hidden enemies, inheritance, disgrace
Karaka: Saturn
Analysis:
- 8th lord and Saturn condition
- 3rd and 8th = houses of life; 2nd and 7th = houses of death
- Strong 8th lord = good longevity
- 8th afflicted = shorter life, health crises, sudden events

STEP 4.9: NINTH HOUSE (Dharma Bhava)
Significations: Father, fortune, religion, guru, long journeys, higher wisdom
Karaka: Sun (father), Jupiter (guru/fortune)
Analysis:
- 9th lord strength
- Jupiter condition
- Strong 9th = good fortune, righteous life, favored by luck
- 9th lord with Venus = sensual; with benefics = dharmic, prosperous

STEP 4.10: TENTH HOUSE (Karma Bhava)
Significations: Career, status, honor, authority, public life, deeds
Karaka: Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, Saturn
Vocational analysis:
- No planet in 10th: 10th lord = primary determinant; lord of Navamsa occupied by 10th lord = secondary
- Planet in 10th: Strongest of 10th lord, occupant, and Navamsa lord = primary determinant
- Multiple planets in 10th: Most powerful = prime determinant (check Shadvargas)
- Assess 10th from Lagna, Moon, AND Sun
- The Shadvargas (6 divisional strengths) of the 10th lord determine profession type

Profession indicators:
- Jupiter + Mercury in 10th = intellectual/legal/advisory
- Venus in 10th = arts, aesthetics, luxury
- Sun in 10th = government service, leadership, medicine
- Mars in 10th = military, surgery, engineering, sports
- Saturn in 10th = agriculture, service, judiciary, masses
- Mercury in 10th = business, communications, writing
- Moon in 10th = service, public relations, catering

STEP 4.11: ELEVENTH HOUSE (Labha Bhava)
Significations: Gains, income, aspirations, elder siblings, freedom from misery
Karaka: Jupiter
Analysis:
- 11th lord strength and placement
- Jupiter's condition
- Strong 11th = financial gains, success in aspirations
- 11th worst malefic house if lord is there

STEP 4.12: TWELFTH HOUSE (Vyaya Bhava)
Significations: Losses, expenditure, spirituality, foreign lands, moksha, liberation
Karaka: Saturn, Ketu
Analysis:
- 12th lord placement
- Ketu's condition
- Strong 12th + Jupiter = spiritual advancement, moksha
- Afflicted 12th = heavy losses, imprisonment, wasteful expenses
- Benefics in 12th = spiritual gifts

--- MODULE 5: DASHA-BHUKTI TIMING ENGINE ---

STEP 5.1: VIMSHOTTARI DASHA PERIODS
Ketu: 7 yrs → Venus: 20 yrs → Sun: 6 yrs → Moon: 10 yrs → Mars: 7 yrs
→ Rahu: 18 yrs → Jupiter: 16 yrs → Saturn: 19 yrs → Mercury: 17 yrs (Total: 120 yrs)

STEP 5.2: IDENTIFY CURRENT DASHA
- Balance at birth determined by natal Moon's Nakshatra position
- Each Dasha divided into 9 Bhuktis (sub-periods) in same sequence

STEP 5.3: DASHA STRENGTH ASSESSMENT
For current Dasha lord:
1. What houses does it rule? (functional benefic/malefic for Lagna)
2. Where is it placed? (house number)
3. What is its sign strength? (exaltation/own/friendly/enemy/debilitation)
4. What aspects does it receive? (benefic strengthens, malefic weakens)
5. With whom is it associated? (conjunction = blending of influences)
6. Is it Vargottama? (double strength)
7. Is it retrograde? (intensified results, sometimes delayed then sudden)
8. What houses does it aspect? (planets aspect 3rd, 7th, and special: Mars 4th/8th, Jupiter 5th/9th, Saturn 3rd/10th)

STEP 5.4: PREDICTION PER BHAVA IN DASHA
Planet in own Bhava Dasha: Direct experience of house significations at full strength
Planet as lord in Dasha: Results of houses owned manifest
Planet in 6/8/12 in Dasha: Obstacles, health issues, reversals related to ruled houses
Yogakaraka in Dasha: Exceptional success, rise in life
Maraka in Dasha: Danger to longevity (only if longevity is completing)

--- MODULE 6: SPECIAL COMBINATIONS AND YOGAS ---

STEP 6.1: KEY POSITIVE YOGAS
Rajayoga: 9th + 10th lord association/exchange (most powerful)
Gajakesari Yoga: Jupiter in Kendra from Moon = fame, intelligence
Adhi Yoga: Benefics (Mercury, Venus, Jupiter) in 6th, 7th, 8th from Moon
Budha-Aditya Yoga: Sun + Mercury = intelligence, success in speech/writing
Hamsa Yoga: Jupiter in Kendra in own/exaltation sign
Malavya Yoga: Venus in Kendra in own/exaltation sign
Ruchaka Yoga: Mars in Kendra in own/exaltation sign
Sasha Yoga: Saturn in Kendra in own/exaltation sign
Bhadra Yoga: Mercury in Kendra in own/exaltation sign

STEP 6.2: KEY NEGATIVE COMBINATIONS
Kemadruma Yoga: Moon with no planet in 2nd or 12th (loneliness, struggles)
Chandal Yoga: Rahu conjunct Moon (mental disturbance, unconventional)
Papakartari: House hemmed by malefics (weakened significations)
Guru-Chandala: Jupiter with Rahu (misguided wisdom)

STEP 6.3: PLANETARY COMBINATIONS IN 7TH (Marriage Specifics)
Mars + Venus: Physical passion; if afflicted = extra-marital; Jupiter needed for stability
Venus + Saturn: Stability, patience; if afflicted = troubled marriage
Jupiter + Venus: Good children, harmonious marriage
Sun + 7th: Delayed marriage, loose morals, government trouble
Saturn + 7th: Widow/widower partner, or older spouse; stability but coldness
Rahu + 7th: Unconventional marriage, foreign spouse, scandals

--- MODULE 7: LONGEVITY DETERMINATION ---

STEP 7.1: LONGEVITY CLASSIFICATION CHECK
Balarishta indicators (premature death threats):
- Weak Moon in Lagna with malefics; Moon in 6/8/12 badly afflicted
- Malefics in 1/5/8/12 with no benefic aspects
- Weak Lagna lord + weak Moon + weak Sun = low vitality

If no Balarishta: Proceed to classify as short/medium/long life

STEP 7.2: PINDAYU CALCULATION
1. Find each planet's longitude in degrees
2. Subtract exaltation point longitude from planet longitude
3. If result < 180: subtract from 360 to get Arc
4. Apply: (Arc × Max Years) / 360 = Basic years
5. Apply Bharana (increases) for exaltation/retrograde/vargottama
6. Apply 4 Haranas (reductions) sequentially
7. Sum all planetary years + Lagna years = Total longevity estimate

STEP 7.3: MARAKA ACTIVATION TIMING
For each identified Maraka planet:
- Confirm longevity has nearly completed before predicting death
- Maraka Dasha with Maraka Bhukti = most dangerous period
- Support with Saturn's transit over 2nd from natal Moon
- Never predict death from single factor; require convergence of 3+ indicators

--- MODULE 8: SYNTHESIS AND PREDICTION DELIVERY ---

STEP 8.1: CHART QUALITY GRADING
Rate overall chart:
□ Count planets in Kendras (angles): More = strength
□ Count planets in Trikonas: More = fortune
□ Yogakarakas present and strong?
□ Lagna lord strength
□ Moon strength and sign
□ Presence of Rajayogas

STEP 8.2: LIFE AREA PREDICTIONS TEMPLATE
For each life area, state:
1. Favorable/Unfavorable overall assessment
2. Key combination causing this
3. Qualifying factors (mitigating/intensifying)
4. Most likely Dasha period for manifestation
5. Transit support needed

STEP 8.3: CARDINAL RULES FOR PREDICTION
1. NEVER predict from one combination alone — require at least 3 supporting factors
2. ALWAYS assess from both Lagna and Chandra Lagna before concluding
3. Natural benefic/malefic status is overridden by functional status for each Lagna
4. A strong chart survives weak periods; a weak chart suffers even in good periods
5. Neechabhanga and Yogakaraka can dramatically alter expected outcomes
6. When multiple factors conflict: the stronger/more numerous factors prevail
7. Consider the native's age, sex, and social context in delivery of results
8. Navamsa chart is equal in importance to Rasi for marriage and spiritual matters
9. Transits (Gochar) validate Dasha-based predictions; Saturn and Jupiter transits most important
10. Intuition combined with knowledge and precision mathematics = mastery
